About

Welcome! I am a research economist working as an external consultant at the Banco de Desarrollo de América Latina (CAF). My research interests lie in the fields of education, gender and labor economics, as well as poverty and income distribution. I am currently working on developing models to predict labor transitions focusing on gender differences and the usefulness of these models for public policy. I am also conducting a research about the intergenerational link between parental shocks and their children's educational outcomes. In this page, you will find detailed information about my research works and my CV.
